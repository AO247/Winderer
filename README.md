# Winderer

> Master the wind. Clear the tower. Become a god.

**Winderer** is a 3D action roguelike where you play as a mage wielding the power of wind. Hurl enemies off island arenas, weaponize the environment, and grow stronger with every fight as you climb to the top of the tower — where the god of the wind awaits.

Built on our **custom in-house engine** powered by DirectX 11.

## Gameplay

You are a wind mage fighting your way up a tower floor by floor. Wind isn't just your weapon — it's your entire toolkit. Throw enemies off the edge, pick up columns and rocks to hurl at them, use fire and destructible elements scattered across each arena. Every fight rewards you with power before the next.

**Key Features:**

- **Dynamic environmental interaction** — columns, rocks, ceramics, fire: everything is a weapon or a shield
- **Varied enemy types** — each enemy demands a different approach and wind-based tactics
- **Randomly generated maps** — no two runs are the same; high replayability by design
- **Skill evolution** — upgrade your basic abilities to fundamentally change how they work, opening new tactical possibilities
- **Tower boss** — reach the top and face the avatar of the wind god in a final confrontation

**Genre:** Action Roguelite · Fighting  
**Tags:** Airbending, Roguelike, 3D
